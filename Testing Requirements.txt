. Testing Requirements
•	Functional Testing:
o	Product Browsing: Test for search accuracy, filter functionality, and load times.
o	Cart Management: Validate add/remove functions, item quantity updates, and checkout process.
o	Order Tracking: Confirm update frequency and location accuracy.
•	Performance Testing:
o	Load Testing: Verify loading time under 2 seconds.
o	Scalability: Test app performance with 10,000 simultaneous users.
•	Security Testing:
o	Payment Gateway: Test for PCI compliance and data encryption.
o	Data Security: Simulate data breaches and validate encryption.
•	Usability Testing:
o	Test navigation ease for browsing, adding to cart, and checking out.
o	Confirm accessibility on different screen sizes and OS versions.
